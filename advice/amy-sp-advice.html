Practice Makes Perfect!

Well, maybe not perfect but good enough to have confidence in your abilities.

Reseach into how the outcomes of practice on performance have found it takes 20 hours of focused deliberate practice to ge reasonably good at something

  1. Deconstruct the skill you want to learn into smaller pieces
  2. Learn enough to self-correct
  3. Remove barriers to practice
  4. Practice for at least 20 hours

If you follow these steps you will be on your way to shedding imposter syndrome, making some money, and creating beautiful functional code!
