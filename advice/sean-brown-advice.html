Hi, my name is Sean and I have some advice for you!

Don't get discouraged

I would say when you start, don't let anything discourage you. Yes, this stuff can be complicated, but you are in good hands.

Your understanding will be built from the ground up. You will learn the best possible practices that are currently being used in the tech industry.

Take notes.

I'm not kidding, this is essential to learning a new skill. If you take notes on your phone or on paper, this is a must especially with all these new terms you will be learning.

I still refer back to some of my very first notes for a quick brain jogger sometimes.

You will not regret taking "detailed" notes!
