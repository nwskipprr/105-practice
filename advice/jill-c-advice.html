Hi, my name is Jillian and I have some advice for you!

Confusion can be the best teacher.

Starting to code can be intimidating. There's a lot to learn and even more to remember.

Confusion, therefore, is a sign that you are heading in the right direction. It means you're doing something right, and overcoming the problem is best way to learn and remember.

Problem-solving is underrated.

Have a problem that you can't seem to solve? Use all your resources: friends, internet, tutorials, even strangers. Because once you solve the problem once, you'll never have to do it again.

Plus, there's nothing like the feeling of getting something done RIGHT.
