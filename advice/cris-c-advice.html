Hi, my name is Cris Crawford and I have some advice for you!

Be clear about your career objective!

Write down what it is that you are trying to accomplish. A new job? A promotion? Freelance work? Every day, envision your goal. Picture yourself doing that work. Imagine how you will feel.

The greater your clarity about your goal, the more likely you are to achieve success.

Work on your goal every single day.

Do something to move closer to your target outcome every single day.

It doesn't matter how small the task that you start with. Day by day you will build a momentum that will propel you towards success.

Each step you take will reveal more about what you will need to do to achieve your goal.

Strive for excellent time management skills

Plan your day every morning

Avoid distractions and fight procrastination.

Review how you managed your time at the end of the day.
