Here is some foxdevilwild advice for you!

Keep going

When you start learning a new language you will inevitably fail. Keep practicing, failing and definitely keep going. At some point you will figure it out and you will learn from your own mistakes.

Ask for help

We are often too scared to ask for help, because we are too worried about what other people will think. Don't forget that everybody started somewhere and ask for help if you feel stuck.
