Time

Most likely you'll need to commit a lot more time than you initially thought. Don't let that put you off - - most of it is fun and not at all like work.

Pace

Try not to be in a hurry, chances are you'll make more mistakes that way. Chillax and proceed leisurely but attentively.

Learning Strategies

Coming from a science background?
3-D and 4-D imagination frameworks simply do not work here. You will have to develop a whole new conceptual framework for learning - - one (or more) that suits you. Good luck !

Practise

Practise, practise and practise. “Tell me and I forget, teach me and I may remember, involve me and I learn.”
― Benjamin Franklin
