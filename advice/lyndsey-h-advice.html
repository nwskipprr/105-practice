Hi, my name is Lynz and I have some advice for you!

Mistakes rock

I'm in the process of transferring from marketing into programming. At first, all my mistakes stressed me out (even though they were inevitable for such a newbie.) That's because mistakes in my previous job were seriously frowned upon.

One of the joys of coding is that every mistake is a lesson, and stops you from doing the same thing again next time.

So although it feels unnatural, it's time to embrace your errors!

Little and often

You're essentially learning a bunch of new languages, so you can't expect to practice for an hour a week. Or even one full day a week!

For me, the only way that my new skills stuck in my brain is showing up every single day, even when you don't feel like it.

Practice makes perfect, after all.
