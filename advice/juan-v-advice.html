Hi, my name is Juan Vazquez and I have some advice for you!

Coding can be intimidating at first, but these do's and dont's will make the process feel a little bit easier!

Do:

  • Take it one task at a time
  • Use every resource you can!
  • Use your browser's inspector tool!

Don't:

  • Let frustration get the best of you
  • Get stuck on the same problem for too long
  • Try to take on too much at once
