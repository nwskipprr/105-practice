Consistency is Key!

Even when things seem to be going over your head, stick with it! That lightbulb moment may be just around the corner. When you persist through a challenge, you may not know when you'll stunble upon the answer that will connect the dots, but it just may be right under your nose!

Break it Down

Stay in the moment and focus on taking small, thoughtful steps towards your bigger goal. Practice makes permanent, and taking one sure step at a time is better than rushing through and getting overwhelmed!
